Our Story

Welcome to S&S Footwear, a passion-driven business developed through teamwork, here in Sri Lanka. It is headed by our co-founders, M.S. Kumararathna and his wife, Suneetha Rajapaksha. Together, their combined initials perfectly form our name, S&S, truly reflecting the personal commitment and joint vision that powers our business. From day one, this vibrant couple made it their mission to create good-quality, comfortable, and stylish footwear, tending to S&S Footwear like members of one's own family, closely understanding and serving the local market. This zeal has led us to become a sought-after Sri Lankan brand.

Our journey, like all ardent endeavors, began with its own set of challenges. M.S. Kumararathna first converted a private residence into our initial workshop, which began with a small team. But early days also brought unforeseen problems: our initial team members moved on, and we were often forced to endure the frustration of not having necessary materials at hand, necessitating much effort to obtain what we had to bring in. These hiccups actually tested our determination hard, but they subsequently pushed our resolve to establish a solid and independent indigenous manufacturing. In addressing these challenges, M.S. Kumararathna and Suneetha Rajapaksha personally undertook running operations, cautiously training new staff, and networking close relationships with local suppliers. This dynamic management and attention to local resources formed the core of S&S Footwear, which allowed us to maintain full control over our production and provide quality consistently.

Today, S&S Footwear is slowly but surely increasing popularity among our valued customers. We attribute this to our unshakable attention to quality and an unapologetically customer-oriented approach. We take the extra step to market our genuine products through readily accessible media like Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp Business, making it easy for you to discover our latest designs. As M.S. Kumararathna and Suneetha Rajapaksha always emphasize, success is in making you happy. We strongly believe that the happy customer's positive word-of-mouth and referral are the strongest and most effective advertising, more so than even the most expensive campaign.
